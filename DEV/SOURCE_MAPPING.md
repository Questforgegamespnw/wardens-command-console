## Transformation Rule

When source content is adapted for runtime:

1. Preserve the mechanical truth.
2. Remove unnecessary narrative text.
3. Use stable IDs.
4. Document any console-specific simplification.
5. Do not treat console data as the upstream canonical source.

## Known Alpha Transformations

- Cover has no tracked Integrity by default.
- Direct Wounds are manually selected.
- Aegis is resolved as a separate target.
- Vehicle and Ship TAC are deferred.
- Quick Entity templates omit full inventory and biography.
